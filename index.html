Portfolio

Allay

Allay is a platform that provides Lambda School students candid company or interview reviews from alumni to prepare the students for job searching.

This app was built with the help of 6 full stack developers as well as 2 UX designers. The team worked fully remote to ship a finished application to the project stake holder. I mainly worked on the front end development. I am most proud of the messenger style form for submitting a review. The idea behind the form was making the interface seem to be live with a person inquiring about feedback. This form is built with timers to trigger new messages as well as unlock more fields for the review to input into.

  • React
  • Redux
  • Chakra UI
  • Node.js
  • Express.js

Custom Create React App Template

This project stemmed from the desire to quickly create a scalable project without spending hours on simply boiler plating. I searched and searched for hours without finding what I was looking for in a currently distributed template. After watching a simple tutorial I started my deep dive into C_R_A and NPM to start this template. I included not only Redux but also a styling package called Chakra UI that is built on tailwind CSS to make styling a breeze. I have plans on continuing to update this template as my needs and desires progress.

About

Just a little about myself.

I am a Full Stack Developer specializing in React, JavaScript and Node.js. Before I became a developer I owned and operated City Limit Recording where I managed a group of audio engineers. While at CLR I mastered the art of self management and perfected prioritizing tasks. During my free time I held various positions at different start up crypto currency companies which lead me to development. I decided to attend Lambda School late 2019 ( an intensive 9 month development school) where I focused on full stack web development. Over the course of the last year I have worked on remote cross functional teams that prepared me to be a vital piece of a development team. I can not wait to see what is next and am welcoming all opportunities!
